About Imaishi hiroyuki

    Imaishi Hiroyuki(今石 洋之, born October 4, 1971) is a Japanese key animator and animation director. He is one of the co-founders of Studio Trigger. Prior to founding Trigger, he was an animator and director at Gainax. Imaishi's style is marked by fast and frantic animation combined with elaborate storyboarding and punchy direction.
